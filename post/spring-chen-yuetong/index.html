SPRING Chen yuetong

发布于 2020-05-15 | 分类于 books | 1分钟 | 149字数

Spring (Part 1)

Spring morning,

It's so cute!

Cool wind,

Flying butterflies,
 
         joyable mood.

Spring (Part 2)

Spring park,

Vibrant everywhere!

warm sunlight,

Colorful flowers,

Green grass.

Spring (Part 3)

Spring slowly approached me,

The sun shines on me,

I lie on the green grass,

Enjoying the good times of spring!

Spring (Part 4)

Where is spring?

The trees became lush,

Hua'er smiled,

The sun is shining and warm.

It turns out that spring has arrived!

Spring (Part 5)

Spring comes slowly,

The grass is green and delicate,

That yellow cauliflower, yellow flourishes,

The blue of that creek, how intoxicating is blue

Spring (Part 6)

Spring wind,

How amazing!

Blow gently

The cherry blossoms are falling,

It seems like a rain of flowers!

Spring (Part 7)

Spring rain,

What a cool block!

Tiny raindrops,

Slowly fall.

Land gently on the ground,

It must be cool and comfortable.
